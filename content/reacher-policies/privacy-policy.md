- **Disconnecting a Connected Platform Account:** When You disconnect a Facebook Page, Instagram Business or Creator Account, Meta Ad Account, TikTok account, or other Connected Platform Account — whether through Reacher or through the platform's own settings — all Platform Data obtained from that account is permanently deleted from Our production systems within 30 days.

- **Backups:** Data may persist in encrypted backups for a limited additional period not exceeding 90 days before being purged on Our standard backup rotation. Backup data is not used for any active processing.
